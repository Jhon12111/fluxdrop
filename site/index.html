FluxDrop

Transfer files between computers
at Wi‑Fi speed

FluxDrop is a free, universal app for Windows and macOS to send files, chat, and make voice calls between computers on the same network — at full link speed, no internet, no cables, no size limits.

Download for WindowsWindows 10 / 11 · 64-bit Download for macOSmacOS 11+ · Universal (Intel & Apple Silicon)

Free & open source · all releases

📡

Automatic discovery

Every computer running FluxDrop on your network appears instantly. No IP addresses, no pairing, no setup.

⚡

Full network speed

Raw TCP streaming saturates gigabit Wi‑Fi and wired links. Move huge video projects in minutes, not hours.

📂

Files & whole folders

Drag & drop anything. Folder structure is preserved exactly — perfect replacement for shuttling SSDs around.

💬

Text chat

Message any device right from its card — with sounds, notifications, and Sent / Seen read receipts.

📞

Voice calls

Call another computer over Wi‑Fi. Crystal-clear peer-to-peer audio with a ringtone, accept / decline, and mute.

🔁

Windows ⇄ Mac

Works between any combination: Windows to Windows, Mac to Mac, and Windows to Mac in both directions.

🛡️

Private by design

Everything travels directly between your computers over your own network. Nothing ever touches the internet.

🌙

Always ready

Keeps running in the tray / menu bar so files, chats, and calls arrive — with a desktop notification — even when the window is closed.

How it works

1

Install FluxDrop on both computers connected to the same Wi‑Fi or LAN.

2

Open the app — the other device appears automatically within seconds.

3

Send, chat, or call — drop files onto the device card, or hit Message / Call to talk.

Windows: on first launch click Allow on the firewall prompt (Private networks). SmartScreen may warn about an unknown publisher — choose “More info → Run anyway”.
macOS: the app is not notarized yet — on first open, right-click the app and choose Open.
